Welcome to What's Your Poison?

What's Your Poison? is a comprehensive cocktail management app that helps you discover, organize, and create amazing drinks. Whether you're a home bartender, cocktail enthusiast, or just looking to make better use of your bar, this app is designed to transform your cocktail experience.

Key Features

  • Smart Bar Management: Track your entire bar inventory with barcode scanning and organized categories
  • Intelligent Cocktail Suggestions: Get personalized recommendations based on what you already have
  • Universal Recipe Import: Import recipes from popular cocktail websites with one click
  • Comprehensive Organization: Filter, search, and organize your cocktail collection
  • Beautiful Interface: Modern design with list and grid views

Getting Started

When you first open the app, you'll be guided through:

  1. Permissions: Grant access to your photo library and camera for adding cocktail images

Quick Start Guide

  1. Add items to your bar using the Bar tab
  2. Use barcode scanning for quick additions
  3. Browse suggested cocktails based on your inventory
  4. Add new cocktails manually or import from websites

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new cocktail?

Tap the "+" button in the top right corner of the Cocktails tab. Fill in the cocktail details including name, ingredients, instructions, base spirit, category, and difficulty. You can also add a photo and set prep time. Tap "Save" when you're done.

How do I import a cocktail from a website?

Tap the "+" button in the Cocktails tab, then select "Import from URL". Paste the recipe URL from a supported website. The app will automatically extract the recipe details for you to review and save. Supported websites include:

Kindred Cocktails Master of Mixes Serious Eats A Couple Cooks Buchanan's Whisky Jim Beam

What base spirits can I choose from?

You can select from:

  • Whiskey/Whisky: Bourbon, Scotch, Irish, American, Tennessee
  • Other Spirits: Vodka, Gin, Rum, Tequila, Brandy, Non-Alcoholic
  • Custom: Select "Other" to enter a custom base spirit name (e.g., "Angostura", "Sloe Gin")

How do I filter cocktails?

Use the filter button in the top left corner of the Cocktails tab. You can filter by:

  • Category (Classic, Modern, Tropical, Martini, Sour, etc.)
  • Base Spirit
  • Difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard)
  • Prep Time
  • Alcohol Content (Non-Alcoholic, Low Alcohol)

How do I manage my bar inventory?

Go to the Bar tab to view and manage your ingredients. You can:

  • Add items manually or by scanning barcodes
  • Organize items by category (Spirits, Liqueurs, Mixers, Garnishes, etc.)
  • Update quantities and expiration dates
  • Remove items you no longer have

How do I switch between list and grid view?

Tap the grid/list icon in the top right corner of the Cocktails tab to toggle between views. In grid view, use the slider icon to adjust the number of columns (1-4).

How do I share a cocktail?

Open any cocktail and tap the share button in the top right corner. You can share via email, message, or print the recipe.

How do I backup my data?

Tap the "+" button in the Cocktails tab, then select "Backup & Restore". Tap "Create New Backup" to save your current cocktails and bar items. The app automatically keeps your 3 most recent backups.

How do I restore from a backup?

Open "Backup & Restore" from the Add menu, select the backup you want to restore, and tap "Restore". Warning: This will replace all your current data with the backup. Make sure this is what you want before confirming.

How many backups can I keep?

The app automatically keeps your 3 most recent backups. When you create a new backup and you already have 3 backups, the oldest one is automatically deleted to make room for the new one.

Troubleshooting

App is not loading cocktails

Try these steps:

  1. Close the app completely (swipe up from the bottom and swipe the app away)
  2. Restart your device
  3. Make sure you have an internet connection (for recipe imports)
  4. Update to the latest version of the app from the App Store
  5. If the problem persists, try creating a backup and reinstalling the app

Can't add photos to cocktails

Make sure you've granted photo access permissions to the app:

  1. Go to Settings on your device
  2. Scroll down and tap "What's Your Poison?"
  3. Enable "Photos" access
  4. If you want to take new photos, also enable "Camera" access

Recipe import from URL isn't working

Some websites require JavaScript to load recipe content, which our app cannot parse. If you see an error message indicating a website requires JavaScript, you'll need to manually enter the recipe.

Supported websites: Kindred Cocktails, Master of Mixes, Serious Eats, A Couple Cooks, Buchanan's Whisky, and Jim Beam.

Tips for better imports:

  • Make sure you're using the full recipe URL, not a shortened link
  • Check that the website is one of our supported sites
  • If import fails, try copying the recipe manually

Barcode scanning isn't working

If barcode scanning isn't working:

  1. Make sure you've granted camera permissions
  2. Ensure good lighting when scanning
  3. Hold the barcode steady and at the right distance
  4. Clean your camera lens
  5. If the item isn't recognized, you can always add it manually

Cocktail suggestions aren't showing

If you're not seeing cocktail suggestions:

  1. Make sure you have items in your Bar inventory
  2. Check that your bar items match ingredient names in cocktails
  3. Try adding more items to your bar for better matches
  4. Some cocktails may require exact ingredient matches

Tips & Tricks

Maximize Your Bar

  • Add all your bar items, including mixers, juices, and garnishes
  • Use barcode scanning for quick additions while shopping
  • Keep your inventory updated to get accurate suggestions
  • Check expiration dates on mixers and juices

Build Your Collection

  • Import recipes from your favorite cocktail websites
  • Save cocktails you want to try later
  • Use favorites to organize your go-to drinks
  • Add photos to make your collection more visual

Get Better Suggestions

  • The more items in your bar, the better the suggestions
  • Use generic names for ingredients (e.g., "Vodka" instead of brand names)
  • Keep your bar inventory current
  • Check the "Missing Ingredients" list to see what you need

Privacy & Data

What's Your Poison? respects your privacy:

  • Local Storage: All your data is stored locally on your device
  • No Tracking: We don't collect or track any personal information
  • No Cloud Sync: Your cocktails and bar inventory stay on your device
  • Backups: Backups are stored locally on your device

The app only requests camera and photo library permissions to allow you to add images to your cocktails. These permissions are optional and the app works perfectly without them.
