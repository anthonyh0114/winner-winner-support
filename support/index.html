Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new recipe?

Tap the "+" button in the top right corner of the Recipes tab. Fill in the recipe details and tap "Save".

How do I filter recipes?

Use the filter button in the top left corner of the Recipes tab. You can filter by cuisine, cooking time, difficulty, and more.

How do I manage my pantry?

Go to the Pantry tab to add, remove, or update your ingredients. The app will suggest recipes based on what you have.

How do I share a recipe?

Open any recipe and tap the share button in the top right corner. You can share via email, message, or print.

Troubleshooting

App is not loading recipes

Try these steps:

  1. Close the app completely
  2. Restart your device
  3. Make sure you have an internet connection
  4. Update to the latest version of the app

Can't add photos to recipes

Make sure you've granted photo access permissions to the app in your device settings.
